Privacy Policy for VoxaNote

Last Updated: December 2025

1. Introduction

VoxaNote ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

Audio Recordings:

  • We collect audio recordings that you create using the app
  • Recordings are stored securely on our servers
  • You can delete recordings at any time through the app

Transcription Data:

  • We process your audio recordings to generate transcriptions
  • Transcriptions are created using AI services (OpenAI)
  • Transcription data is stored along with your recordings

Account Information:

  • Account creation is optional - you can use the app without creating an account
  • If you create an account, we collect a user identifier to associate your recordings
  • We may collect device information for app functionality and troubleshooting

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide transcription and AI-powered features
  • To generate summaries and insights from your recordings
  • To enable chat functionality about your recordings
  • To improve our services

4. Data Storage and Security

Your recordings and data are stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use secure cloud storage services to protect your information.

5. Third-Party Services

We use OpenAI's services for transcription, summarization, and AI chat features. Your audio data and transcripts are processed by OpenAI in accordance with their privacy policy. We use cloud storage services (S3-compatible) to securely store your recordings. We do not share your data with other third parties for marketing purposes.

6. Your Rights

  • You can access your recordings and data at any time
  • You can delete recordings and data through the app
  • You can request deletion of your account and all associated data

7. Data Retention

We retain your recordings and data until you delete them or request account deletion. Deleted data is permanently removed from our systems.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date.
